Upgrading existing solution for your business.

As businesses grow, it is usually the case that the existing systems begin to fall short of expectations. And setting aside a big budget for a completely new solution may not be feasible for all.

Hence this case calls for a careful strategy that achieves the goal to move to a solution that can stand the test of current demands of the business, and yet remain financially and chronologically efficient for the new change to be incorporated as part of the current business processes.

The key here is not implementing the best solution but instead a solution that is better than the previous one in the current situation.
